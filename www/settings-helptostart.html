Help to Start

Help to start a movement by starting your day with 8 minutes in God’s presence and encouraging others to do the same.  Share creative ideas to encourage your friends to start their day with God:

  • Meet at the same time each morning;
  • Text your friends with the scripture or other things the Lord said to you that morning;
  • Share your morning image/picture;
  • Ask God for a word of encouragement for your friend/s;
  • Over coffee or a meal, share what God is speaking to you about;
  • Share music and songs the Lord reminded you of that morning; and
  • Encourage each other in the ways you want to be more Christ like.
