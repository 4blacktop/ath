About

Our heart is to ‘bring good news to the poor, to bind up the brokenhearted, bring freedom for the captives and release from darkness for the prisoners.’ Currently, we are:

Serving the poor in India – we distributed 35,000 mosquito nets in 2016 and we plan to dig a well in 100 villages in 2017 as well as giving another 15,000 mosquito nets and taking a medical team to do things like immunizations, cataract operations etc.

We are comforting people who are suffering with the comfort that God has given to us to bring:

  • Healing to the brokenhearted;
  • Hope to the depressed;
  • Help and healing to people suffering from the impact of suicide;
  • Advice and guidance for marriages, especially those in crisis; and
  • Answers for people interested in the afterlife.

ATG is a non-profit organization and funds from the ‘Awakening to God Today’ App will go towards providing clean water and other help to those in poverty in India.
